Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Empty Ring ("the App") is a fasting timer application developed as an indie project. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect - and in short, we collect none.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App functions entirely on your device. No account is required. No analytics are gathered. No third-party SDKs are included.

Local Storage

The App stores your fasting preferences, session history, and streak data locally on your device using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock Premium features. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the support page.
