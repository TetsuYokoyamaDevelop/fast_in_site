Fast In - Fasting Timer

How to use

Choose your fasting protocol (e.g. 16:8), then tap Start to begin your fast. The circular progress ring shows your remaining fasting time. When the fasting window ends, the eating window begins automatically. Complete the full cycle to record a streak.

Frequently asked questions

What fasting protocols are available?
The free version includes 16:8. Upgrading to Premium unlocks 18:6, 20:4, OMAD (23:1), and a custom protocol where you can set 1-23 hours.

Does the timer work in the background?
Yes. The timer uses real-time calculation, so it stays accurate even if you close the app or lock your screen. You'll receive a notification when your fast is complete.

Does the screen stay on during fasting?
Yes. The screen stays awake while the timer is active so you can glance at your progress.

How do streaks work?
Each completed fasting cycle counts as one streak day. The free version shows your last 7 days. Premium unlocks full history with a calendar view.

Can I change the color?
Yes. The free version offers Green and Blue. Premium unlocks all 8 theme colors.

Is there a subscription?
No. Fast In is free to use with a one-time optional purchase to unlock all Premium features. No recurring charges.

Does the app collect any data?
No. See our Privacy Policy for details.
